Our Story

A Passion for Unforgettable Flavors

Nikko was born from a love of honest cooking and warm hospitality. Our chefs partner with local farms and fisheries to bring you dishes that celebrate the season — bold, refined, and always from the heart.

Every evening, our candlelit dining room becomes a stage for flavors that surprise and delight, paired with wines selected from boutique vineyards around the world.
